# Figma Make Prompt – Ultra Premium Hero Section for MD General Physician Website

Design a hero section for an MD General Physician's website that feels like it was designed by the team behind Apple, Stripe, and a luxury private healthcare brand.

The goal is for visitors to immediately think:

**"This doctor is highly credible, experienced, approachable, and premium."**

Do not design a generic medical website hero section. Avoid stock healthcare layouts, split-screen templates, and cliché doctor cards.

The hero section should feel cinematic, sophisticated, and editorial.

---

## Overall Feeling

Imagine:

Apple Product Launch × Luxury Hotel Website × Private Healthcare Brand × High-End Editorial Landing Page.

The section should feel:

* Calm
* Expensive
* Trustworthy
* Human
* Confident
* Sophisticated
* Minimal

The design should rely heavily on:

* Exceptional spacing
* Large typography
* Composition
* Depth
* Premium photography presentation

---

# Hero Layout

Desktop height:
100vh

Maximum content width:
1440px

Padding:
Left and right padding: 80px
Top padding: 120px

Do not use a strict 50/50 split.

Instead create an asymmetrical composition:

Left content:
45%

Right visual:
55%

The right visual should dominate slightly because the doctor is the product and should become the focal point.

---

# Background

The background should not be plain white.

Create layers:

Layer 1:
Off-white base (#FAFAFA)

Layer 2:
Very subtle radial blue gradient behind the doctor image.

Layer 3:
Extremely soft floating light effects.

Layer 4:
Large blurred organic shapes.

Think:
Apple event stage lighting.

Everything should feel airy.

No medical illustrations.
No stethoscope graphics.
No heart icons.

---

"Comprehensive Healthcare for You and Your Family."

Avoid.

Use an emotional and editorial headline.

Expertise that heals.

or

or

Trusted care for every stage of life.

SF Pro Display

88px

Three lines.

Some words should use accent color.

Expertise that

# Right Side Visual

This is the hero's masterpiece.

Do not simply place a rectangular doctor photo.

Create an artistic composition.

---

# Doctor Portrait Presentation

Use a professionally cut-out image.

Position:
Bottom aligned.

Scale:
Large.

The doctor should occupy almost 80% of the visual area.

The face should naturally align near the headline height.

---

# Doctor Frame

Behind the doctor create a modern architectural frame.

Shape:
Tall rounded arch.

Not rectangle.
Not circle.

Think:
Luxury hotel doorway.

Dimensions:
Large vertical arch.

Background:
Gradient from white to soft blue.

Border:
Very subtle translucent border.

Shadow:
Extremely soft.

This arch becomes the stage for the doctor.

---

# Background Glow

Behind the arch:

Large blurred blue radial glow.

Opacity:
Very low.

Feels like natural light.

Add subtle light rays.

No flashy effects.

---

# Orbital Elements

Around the doctor:

Thin orbital lines.

Very subtle.

Tiny glowing dots.

Inspired by:
Apple Vision Pro marketing.

These elements should communicate:

Precision
Care
Modern medicine

Do not make it look like science fiction.
